# openuz

Free open-source resource with textbook and other learning materials to learn Uzbek.

## Overview

This repository is part of a series of open-source language learning resources. Our goal is to provide high-quality, free, and accessible materials for anyone wanting to master Uzbek.

## Contents

- **Textbook**: Comprehensive structured learning material.
- **Exercises**: Practice problems and quizzes to reinforce learning.
- **Audio Resources**: Pronunciation guides and listening practice.
- **Vocabulary Lists**: Curated lists of essential words and phrases.


---
*Openuz is a community-driven initiative for free language education.*
